About Us

Based in beautiful Gananoque, Ontario, Blackout Demolition & Landscaping is a locally owned company specializing in Interior Demolition projects, as well as small to medium property maintenance.

Servicing the Trenton to Cornwall corridor, Blackout has serviced a number of new and existing clients over the past 2 years with highly rated standards and fair prices.
